Privacy Policy

Echo Hunter

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the "Echo Hunter" application created by Alok Kumar. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

2. Information Collection and Use

This application operates entirely offline and does not use advertisements, analytics SDKs, or any third-party tracking services.

We do not knowingly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

Ultra-Low Size & Zero Permissions: Echo Hunter is designed as a native, lightweight application (under 50KB). It does not request or require internet access permissions.

Local Gameplay: All game logic, state saving, and score progression occur directly on your device using local storage. No gameplay data is ever uploaded to external servers.

3. Log Data

Because the app operates completely offline and lacks network connectivity, we do not collect or receive Log Data over the internet. In the rare case of an app crash, generic error data is only collected if you explicitly choose to share it through Android's built-in, secure system reporting functionality provided by Google Play Services.

4. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Given the offline nature of the game, no data is collected from users of any age.

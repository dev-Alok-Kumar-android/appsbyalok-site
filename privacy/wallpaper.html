Privacy Policy

Dynamic Live Wallpaper

Summary: "Dynamic Live Wallpaper" is a completely offline application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

1. Introduction

Alok Kumar ("we," "our") developed the Dynamic Live Wallpaper app as a Free application. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any user data. The app operates entirely on your device and lacks the capability to transmit data to external servers.

3. Permissions

  • SET_WALLPAPER: Required to change the home/lock screen background.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Optional. Only used if you choose to set a custom image from your gallery.
